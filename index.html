Innohack is a three-day hackathon organised by Amal Jyothi College of Engineering, Kanjirapally and SRH Berlin University of Applied science. The hackathon aims at testing and developing the problem-solving skills of young minds. Participants will be provided with 4 themes to choose from for which an abstract has to be prepared. After judgement, teams with the most innovative and promising solutions will be shortlisted for the three day hackathon. After completion of presentation of solutions, winners of the respective countries will be awarded with a ten day visit to India and Germany.

Rules and Regulations

  • Indian and German under/post graduate students of the age 18 or above are eligible to participate in the event.
  • Teams of 2 should be formed. Those interested in collaboration with the other country can have 4 members in a team, wherein 2 participants must upload the abstract of the solution.
  • All the participants must upload the abstract of the solution.
  • Participants should agree the terms and conditions of innohack.
  • Shortlisting of top 35 teams will be based on abstract submitted.
  • Final round will be held in the offine mode. Shortlisted students will have to attend the same in their corresponding organising colleges.
